# Welcome to Hives & Colonies

Hi, I’m William — an independent developer creating Minecraft mods inspired by my daughter’s ideas, stories, and dreams.

What started as a shared imagination project has slowly grown into **Hives & Colonies**, a Minecraft experience about building a home, growing a colony, discovering magic, caring for bees, and meeting characters who feel like they truly belong in the world.

Right now, I’m working on two connected parts of the project.

## Hives & Colonies

**Hives & Colonies** is a Minecraft 1.21.1 modpack focused on colony building, magic, bees, automation, exploration, and guided progression.

It brings together systems like **MineColonies**, **Ars Nouveau**, **Productive Bees**, **Create**, and other mods into a more connected adventure where every step feels like part of building something meaningful.

## Hives & Colonies: Characters

**Hives & Colonies: Characters** is the narrative side of the project.

Its first character is **Polen**, a quiet and persistent companion who is not meant to be just a pet, follower, or scripted NPC.

Polen remembers places, reacts to danger, builds trust over time, follows her own routines, expresses emotions, and slowly reveals pieces of her story.

## My Goal

My goal is to create Minecraft experiences that feel warm, alive, and personal — the kind of worlds where progression is not just about power, but about belonging, memory, care, and discovery.

This project is still in early development, which means there will be changes, experiments, bugs, balance updates, rewritten quests, new ideas, and plenty of learning along the way.
